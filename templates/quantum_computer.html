Play against a quantum computer

This time you will actually play against an actual quantum computer. You will play against one of the IBM Quantum Computers.

In order to do that, you will have to submit your flip or not flip choice previously.

You will also have to choose which backend to use. Either a simulator (fast) or an actual quantum computer (slow). If you don't get an imminent result with the quantum computer: This is normal. We first have to schedule this stupid computation on very high demand high end machines. This may take up to several minutes.



Attention: This may possibly take a while.
