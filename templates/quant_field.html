Let's play a bit of coin toss!

Consider two Players: A(lice) and B(ob) and a coin (a strange coin, but I will explain that later).

As can be seen above; the process works by the following

  • The coin starts on heads
  • Alice can flip the coin or leave it that way
  • Bob can flip the coin or leave it that way
  • Alice can flip the coin or leave it that way
  • If the coin is on heads, Alice wins, if it's on tails Bob wins.

No party can see the others move. You play Bob. The computer is Alice.
